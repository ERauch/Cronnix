Localisation de CronniX

Thanks for all the offers to localize CronniX. As you'll notice, at this time CronniX only includes English localization. I will always start by releasing an English version first and then putting the additional language packs on the webpage for separate download.

Please contact me before you start any localization of CronniX. There might often already be a translation (or parts of one) that's just not included in CronniX yet.

The following languages are forseen for CronniX 2:

  • Flemish
  • French
  • German
  • Italian
  • Japanese
  • Korean
  • Portuguese
  • Spanish
  • Swedish
  • Welsh

I'm looking forward to seeing CronniX run in all of those languages. Please don't hesitate to contact me even though your language is already on the list.
